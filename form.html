2. Collection and Storage of Personal Data

Our website is a static website and generally does not collect personal data from visitors unless they actively contact us. No automatic storage or processing of personal data takes place through server-side applications.

a) When Visiting the Website

Our static website is hosted by a hosting provider. The hosting provider may automatically store certain technical information in server log files, including: date and time of access, name and URL of the retrieved file, referring website, browser and operating system used, and name of the internet service provider. This data is used solely for statistical purposes and to ensure technical operation of the website.

b) When Using the Contact Form

If you contact us via the contact form, the data you enter is processed via Formspree — your message is transmitted to and stored on Formspree's servers. Processing is based on Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR. For more information see: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

3. Sharing of Data

As our website is static and offers no user accounts or personalised services, no personal data is passed on to third parties unless required to handle an inquiry or mandated by law.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our static website does not use cookies or tracking technologies such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: request information about your processed personal data (Art. 15 GDPR); request correction or completion of stored data (Art. 16 GDPR); request deletion of your data (Art. 17 GDPR); request restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR); object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR); receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (Art. 20 GDPR); and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR).

6. Data Security

Our static website uses modern security measures to protect your data. Since no data processing takes place on our website, there is no elevated risk of data misuse.

7. Retention Period

Technical data is stored only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. After fulfilling the respective purpose, data is anonymised or deleted.

8. Third-Party Content

Third-party content (e.g. YouTube videos, Google Maps, Instagram) may be embedded in this website. Providers of such content require your IP address to deliver it to your browser. We strive to use only providers that use IP addresses solely for content delivery and have no influence over whether providers store IP addresses for statistical purposes.

9. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or new services. The current version is always available on our website.

Cancellation Policy Stornierungsbedingungen

Cancellation Deadline

Appointments and workshop bookings may be cancelled free of charge up to 48 hours before the scheduled start time.

Late Cancellations

Cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance will be charged at 50% of the session fee.

No-Shows

Failure to attend without prior notice will be charged at 100% of the session fee.

How to Cancel

Please cancel by email at s2m@ionio.de or by phone at +49 152 21319336. Cancellations are confirmed once you receive a written acknowledgement from us.

Payment

Payment is due within 7 days of receiving your confirmation email. If you register for a workshop taking place within 7 days, payment is due within 24 hours of your confirmation email.

Stornierungsfrist

Termine und Workshop-Buchungen können bis 48 Stunden vor dem geplanten Beginn kostenlos storniert werden.

Späte Stornierungen

Stornierungen, die weniger als 48 Stunden im Voraus erfolgen, werden mit 50 % der Sitzungsgebühr berechnet.

Nichterscheinen

Nichterscheinen ohne vorherige Benachrichtigung wird mit 100 % der Sitzungsgebühr in Rechnung gestellt.

So stornieren Sie

Bitte stornieren Sie per E-Mail unter s2m@ionio.de oder telefonisch unter +49 152 21319336. Stornierungen gelten als bestätigt, sobald Sie eine schriftliche Bestätigung von uns erhalten.

Zahlung

Die Zahlung ist innerhalb von 7 Tagen nach Erhalt Ihrer Bestätigungsmail fällig. Bei Anmeldung zu einem Workshop, der in weniger als 7 Tagen stattfindet, ist die Zahlung innerhalb von 24 Stunden nach Ihrer Bestätigungsmail fällig.

*You will get a confirmation mail within 24h. Du erhältst eine Bestätigungsmail innerhalb von 24 Stunden.
