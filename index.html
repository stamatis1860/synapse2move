Neuroathletic Training Neuroathletiktraining

Neuroathletic Training (NAT) is a training method that improves athletic performance and supports rehabilitation by targeting and enhancing the brain and nervous system through movement and sensory input.

Neuroathletiktraining (NAT) ist eine Trainingsmethode, die gezielt das Gehirn und Nervensystem durch Bewegung und sensorische Reize stärkt, um sportliche Leistung zu steigern und Rehabilitation wirksam zu unterstützen.

  • Free 20 min Call Kostenloses 20-Min-Gespräch

    During this 20-minute call, we'll talk about your goals, the challenges you're facing, and your current physical and mental state.   • Neuroassessment -
    Functional Analysis of Your Nervous System     Neuroassessment -
    Funktionale Analyse deines Nervensystems

    This assessment provides a detailed analysis of your central and peripheral nervous system function. By identifying potential inefficiencies or imbalances, we gain valuable insights into how your nervous system influences movement, cognition, and overall performance laying the foundation for targeted interventions if needed.

    Dieses Assessment liefert eine detaillierte Analyse der Funktionalität deines zentralen und peripheren Nervensystems. Durch die Identifizierung potenzieller Ineffizienzen oder Ungleichgewichte gewinnen wir wertvolle Erkenntnisse darüber, wie dein Nervensystem Bewegung, Kognition und Performance beeinflusst – und legen damit die Grundlage für gezielte Interventionen.

    Whether you're looking to improve strength, endurance, coordination, or overall fitness, these training sessions are tailored to your individual goals. Using a neurocentric approach, we integrate targeted exercises that not only train your body but also engage and optimize your nervous system, leading to more efficient, sustainable results.

    Egal, ob du deine Kraft, Ausdauer, Koordination oder allgemeine Fitness verbessern möchtest – diese Trainingseinheiten sind auf deine individuellen Ziele zugeschnitten. Mit einem neurozentrischen Ansatz integrieren wir gezielte Übungen, die nicht nur deinen Körper trainieren, sondern auch dein Nervensystem ansprechen und optimieren, was zu effizienteren und nachhaltigeren Ergebnissen führt.

About Me Über mich

Hi, I'm a sports scientist and certified Neuroathletic Trainer with a deep passion for movement and how the human brain works. My journey into neuroscience began with the Feldenkrais Method, which opened my eyes to how movement and awareness shape performance, regeneration, and creativity. After completing my Bachelor's degree in Sport Science, I dove into the world of neuroathletics with Z-Health — fascinated by how targeted brain-based training can unlock new levels of physical and mental potential. I now integrate this approach not only in performance and rehab settings but also in my own artistic work as a dancer and creative. I'm active in triathlon, tennis, calisthenics and really, anything that challenges my body and mind. Above all, I love to dance. I run with my partner a small dance school focused on Brazilian Zouk, and I truly believe that dancing is one of the best workouts you can do for your body and your brain. Whether in training, therapy, or the creative process, my goal is to help people move smarter, feel stronger, and live more fully. If you want to get to know me better click on my personal Instagram account.
